Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I groom my pet?

Most dogs need grooming every 4-8 weeks depending on breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Cats typically need grooming every 6-12 weeks. We can provide a personalized schedule during your first visit.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We have non-slip floors, secure grooming tables, use pet-safe products only, and all groomers are certified in pet first aid and CPR. We also monitor pets closely throughout the grooming process.

Do you groom aggressive or anxious pets?

Yes, we have experience with pets of all temperaments. We use gentle handling techniques and can take breaks as needed. For severely anxious pets, we may recommend scheduling during quieter hours.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring proof of current vaccinations, any special shampoos or products if your pet has allergies, and a leash or carrier. You may also bring your pet's favorite treats if they have dietary restrictions.
